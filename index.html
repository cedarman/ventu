Clinton,Connecticut

Posted by Cedarman, January 17th, 2019

Clinton is a nice and quiet village on the southeastern coast of Connecticut, 100 miles from New York, in Middlesex County. The inhabitants are around 10000 which become many more during the summer season. It borders to the south with Madison, to the north with Westbrook and to the west with Killingworth. The form of government and that of the First Selectman with its Board. There is a local post office, a fire station, a police station and a large commercial outlet.You can also relax in the nearby Hammonaset Park, a nature reserve, which takes its name from the ancient name of Connecticut.
